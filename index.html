Wins + Learnings

Why this matters for Ellis

  • Fits MTSS: short-cycle intervention with built-in progress monitoring.
  • Supports inclusive practices: expands access to rigorous learning experiences for students with ADHD, math anxiety.
  • Maximizes instructional minutes: calmer sessions increase time-on-task during learning blocks.
  • Positive student demand signal: students asked for more learning time.

Refinements for Weeks 2-4

  1. 1Add more arithmetic manipulatives for students who need additional supports.
  2. 2Keep students seated for the first 30 mins of VR (focus + safety + personal space).
  3. 3Set clear, student-level progress goals (MTSS-ready, easy to track).

Program Goals

  • Engagement: "bored + afraid" → "immersed + excited" about math
  • Foundations: Master gateway skills → unlock grade-level progress (MCAS-aligned)
