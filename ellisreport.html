Class Context

  • Cohort: 4th Grade Success Bound Academy (SBA)
  • Students: 10 started, 7 completed the pilot
  • When: MTSS block - Tue / Thu / Fri (4 weeks)
  • Format: 20 min non-VR + 30 min VR (foundation / gateway skills)

Program Goals

  • Access: Support students with ADHD, Autism, Emotional Disability, and Intellectual Disability → reveal potential that traditional formats can miss
  • Engagement: "bored + afraid" → "immersed + excited" about math
  • Foundations: Master gateway skills → unlock grade-level progress (MCAS-aligned)

Summary & Next Steps

MathStar is built to gift the wonder of math to kids who've learned to fear it. By that measure, this pilot delivered — and we're proud of what your students and team achieved together.

We excelled at what we came to do: engagement, motivation, and regulation. But Ellis's priorities also include classroom transfer, MTSS documentation, and IEP alignment — none of which we measured or set out to address.

That gap leads to the one question that determines everything next: does Ellis see math engagement as a foundation worth investing in its own right — or does any program also need to show a direct impact on performance to justify the spend?

Honestly, your answer is the most valuable thing we could take from this pilot. Whatever you're thinking — please take 5 minutes and tell us.
